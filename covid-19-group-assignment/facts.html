Facts About Covid-19 and How You Can Protect Yourself

Covid-19 has put the entire world in shock. Here are the facts you need to know and how you can protect yourself..

  • As of May 29, 2020, there are about 20,000 new cases and over 1,000 daily in the United States alone

  • Some people may be infected without presenting any symptoms

  • Virus spreads more efficiently than the flu

  • The disease can be spread from an infected person’s, including those that are not showing any symptoms, respiratory droplets. These droplets come from sneezing, coughing, or talking.

  • Wearing a mask helps prevent one from spreading or being able to breath in these droplets

  • Maintaining a social distance of at least 6 feet can prevent one from breathing in droplets

  • Washing your hands with soap and water for 20 seconds eliminates the virus and protects you and others from getting the virus

  • Anyone can be infected by the disease

  • Older people and people with underlying health conditions are at a greater risk of serious complications from the virus

  • Symptoms include fever, dry coughing and shortness of breath. Seek medical attention immediately if you have chest pain, trouble breathing, or bluish face and lips. This is not a full list of symptoms, please consult a medical professional for any concerns regarding symptoms.
